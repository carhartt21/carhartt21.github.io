About

I am a doctoral researcher in the Virtual Worlds and Digital Games Group at Technische Universität Ilmenau, supervised by Prof. Dr. Wolfgang Broll.

My research focuses on comprehensive outdoor scene understanding through multi-scale neural networks. I work on semantic segmentation of natural outdoor environments, 360° panoramic image analysis, sky and cloud segmentation, traffic sign detection in equirectangular imagery, and weather-robust perception through advanced data augmentation strategies.
